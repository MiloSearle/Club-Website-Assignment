THE CLUB

Senior Cricket

There are two senior cricket teams hat play on Saturdays. 1st XI plays in Division 4 of Norfolk Cricket Alliance and the A Team play in Level 4 West of the Norfolk Cricket League. There are also 3 other teams in the Senior Cricket category which includes a midweek T20 team, a Sunday friendly team and an Over 40s team who play in the middle of the week.

Youth Cricket

Youth Cricket has hardball teams which include under 9, 11, 13, 15 and 17 age groups. For these groups, a session is held ever friday night at 6:30pm until 8pm which is led by our coaches.

An under 9 softball section is also run with a All Stars Cricket with 5-8 year olds and Dynamos Cricket with 8-11 year olds on Sundays at 10am to 11am.
